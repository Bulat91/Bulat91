### 🧬 Professional AI & Robotics Focus

I am a Senior Research Engineer specializing in the intersection of **Computer Vision**, **Robotics**, and **Industrial AI**. My work focuses on bridging the gap between advanced academic research and practical, high-stakes industrial applications, specifically in non-destructive testing (NDT) and autonomous systems.

#### 🚀 Key Project: CHIMERA (Autonomous Pressure Vessel Inspection)
I lead the development of the AI assessment module for the **CHIMERA crawler**, a unique robotic system designed for the autonomous inspection of pressure vessels.
- **ASME Standards Integration:** Developed AI modules to assess fitness-for-service based on rigorous ASME industrial standards.
- **Sensor Fusion & ROS:** Integrated multi-modal data from unique ultrasound sensor arrays within the **Robot Operating System (ROS)** framework.
- **Defect Detection:** Built machine learning systems for the autonomous interpretation and classification of structural cracks using **Total Focusing Method (TFM)** ultrasound images.
- **Dataset Curation:** Established and maintain a massive, unique industrial dataset for training high-fidelity NDT models.

#### 🍎 Agricultural Robotics & Instance Segmentation
During my research at Plymouth University and my work with Fieldwork Robotics, I specialized in:
- **Deep Learning for Agriculture:** Developing RGB-based vision systems for the localization and classification of soft fruits (raspberries) in unstructured environments.
- **Instance Segmentation:** Implementing state-of-the-art models for real-time robotic interaction.
- **Pipeline Optimization:** Full-cycle management of data collection, cleaning, model training, and deployment.
